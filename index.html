Mahatma Gandhi

Father of the Nation – Apostle of Peace

Mahatma Gandhi was a great freedom fighter and a symbol of peace and non-violence. He led India to independence through truth, simplicity, and courage.

His movements like the Salt March and Quit India Movement showed the power of peaceful protest. He believed that change could be achieved without violence.

Gandhi’s life continues to inspire people around the world to stand for justice, equality, and humanity.
